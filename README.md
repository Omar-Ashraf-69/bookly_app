## Introduction

Welcome to Bookly, your ultimate destination for exploring and shopping for books! Bookly is designed to provide a seamless experience for browsing, previewing, and discovering your next favorite reads. While it currently doesn't implement a payment functionality, it offers a preview of what's to come in future updates.

## Key Features

- **Book Catalog:** Browse through a curated collection of books across various genres.
  
- **Preview Mode:** Preview books to get a glimpse of their content before making a purchase.
  
- **MVVM Pattern:** Implemented for efficient data management and separation of concerns.
  
- **Flutter_bloc:** Utilized for state management, ensuring a smooth and responsive user interface.
  
- **Restful API:** Integrated for real-time book data updates and enhanced browsing experience.
  
- **Splash Screen:** Welcomes users with a visually appealing splash screen upon app launch.

## Future Updates

While Bookly currently focuses on book exploration and previewing, stay tuned for future updates that may include a payment functionality and additional features to enhance your shopping experience.
